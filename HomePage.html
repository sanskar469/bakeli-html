ABOUT US

That lip-smacking chocolate pastry, you wanted to have for a while, went out of order as soon as you entered the store after a tiring day! We
know your heart broke into pieces. But worry not; we have got a perfect way out to mend your broken heart! Bakeli is a mobile-based
aggregator platform that allows bakery lovers to connect with their nearby bakery stores.

WHAT WE DO

We help you to connect with the nearby bakery stores. Not only that, but we also bring you bakery products from
home-based bakers.
Why home-based bakers? Believe us, besides bakery stores, home-based bakers assure freshly baked items with
good quality. From different types of bread, cookies, pastries to yummy cakes all have a homely touch.
We partner with the hidden gems of your locality (of whom you may not even know!) and present their savouries on
our app. Once their products are on our platform, consumers can relish their fresh baked goods, as ‘Bakeli is always
Freshli’!

HOW 'BAKELI IS ALWAYS FRESHLI?'

Are you planning a surprise birthday party for your best friend or planning your anniversary? Simply start browsing the
nearby bakers/bakery stores on our platform, and then select the creamy strawberry cake, the delicious black forest
with cherry toppings, the yummy red velvet cake, or any other cake that catches your eye. Then choose a date and
time convenient to you, pick up your order, and VOILA!

Even if you are planning a get-together with your friends at your home, you can pre-order the Berries and Cream Tart,
the Lemon Tart, or other delicacies for the day. Even before heading up for a tiring Monday, start with loaves of bread
such as Whole Wheat, Multigrain, Brioche!

Bakeli helps you connect with stores that provide vegan bread, such as Sourdough, Pita, Ezekiel, etc. This has been
done keeping in mind the diverse choices of customers. Apart from assuring good health, these will also lighten up
your mood! So be it doughnuts, muffins, bagels, buns, or cakes, you can get every bakery product, as per your mood,
with the process as simple as mentioned above.
